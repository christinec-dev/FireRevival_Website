Outreach and Crusade Giving

Spread His Gospel

Join us by lending a hand at our open-air campaigns and food schemes. With your seed that you sow, you will also give to the poor and needy communities that we reach out to.

Many communities are struggling with poverty and unemployment. As we feed them spiritually, we also want to feed them physically.

One of the best highlights is seeing a greatful heart for giving someone who doesn’t have anybody who cares about them food & toiletries, or even second-hand supplies like clothing and toys.

Wether you donate, or volunteer with us on event days, your contribution helps us to bless those who are praying for a miracle.

Lend A Hand

Equipment Maintanance

Going out into the field and preaching the Gospel is quite costly. That is why we need your help with maintaining our vehicles and generators on a regular basis.

Maintenance of vehicles include the replacement of tires, brakes and the like needs to be done in order for us to reach those who haven’t heard the good news of the Gospel.

We also need your help to maintain our sound equipment that we need to reach every home in townships across South Africa. Speakers need to be cleaned, and wires needs to be repaired. Generators need to be serviced aswell, in order to function efficiently

Your seed can help us do the work to the best ability and gather souls for the Kingdom of God.

Administration & Fundraising

Running Admin Office

We also need your help with fundraisers to help aid our Ministry to pay for Internet & Data Bundles, Fuel, Printer Ink, Stationary, Food & Clothing Parcels and Maintenance. This can be done via helping us with calls, emails, social media campaigns and the sending of newsletters.

You can also offer your talents by aiding us in the writing, filing and submission of reports, SARS tax returns (NPC's still needs to submit), and many other important documentation and reports that needs to be done in order for the admin of the Ministry to be completed on a monthly and yearly basis.
